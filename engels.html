Willem van de Velde & Son

Willem van de Velde & Son Willem van de Velde the Elder and his son Willem van de Velde the Younger were leaders in the seventeenth-century marine painting and inspired many generations of painters after them. Now they are few known to the general public. This year that will change: the Van de Veldes will be once and for all always put on the map.
